About This Portal


This portal provides students with easy access to course syllabi, regulations, and semester-wise materials. By selecting a course, you can quickly navigate through its folders and view relevant documents. The goal is to make academic resources organized and accessible in one place. Beyond just syllabi, the portal also serves as a centralized hub for academic planning. Students can explore regulations to understand program requirements, review semester content to prepare for classes, and stay aligned with their academic journey. Our vision is to simplify learning by bringing all essential resources together, ensuring that every student can focus more on their studies and less on searching for materials.
